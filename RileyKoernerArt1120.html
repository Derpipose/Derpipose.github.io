Art 1120 Showcase

Name: Riley Koerner

Details: This is a showcase of all of the work that I did for my Summer 2023 Art 1120 class. You can see all of the sketches and final works in the gallery below. If you want to see some fun information on a piece, hover over it to view the flavor text. There may be some minor bugs here and there in the website as this was something that I did code myself. Please forgive me if things don't look quite right.

Graduation Map
This is my personal graduation map. You can see the different semesters that I have left to take over the next two school years before I graduate in 2025.
Blank Graduation Map
This was the base artwork that I created for the graduation map. The clouds acted nicely for semester dividers.
Illusion of space sketch
This was the sketch that I had done after going on a walk and taking some pictures of different areas that I thought I may want to use for my illusion of 3D space assignment. I thought about the different things that make up the illusion of 3D space within 2D surfaces and came up with this kind of idea of a raven looking curiously at a box.
Illusion of space
This is the final version of the Illusion of space project. I added in a few characters, manipulating their size to help give the illusion of drawn space.
Icon Sketches
This was a draft of the icons that I thought I could do up. I did several with each prompt, thinking over each carefully before I decided on one of each.
Icons Final
These were the final icons that I sketched and very carefully lined out. They could still use a bit of revision, but this was what I decided to go with in the end.
Jack and Jill Sketches
This assignment had us taking Wingdings to create a story. The story in question was Jack and Jill. We needed to do this using only the wingdings. These were the sketches I came up with.
