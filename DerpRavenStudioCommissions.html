This is the rules and such for my commissions. I know no one likes to read these so I will keep it brief.

Thank you for looking at me for some art! I am honored as I know I am still growing and learning.

Payment: I only accept Paypal, Kofi, Venmo, or on the rare occasion, Discord Nitro. I demand half payment up front, after consultation, with the second half due by completion. If you are asking for a full color and shading piece, I need the second half of payment upon approval of the line art. If you can't pay right away, I will bump your commission spot back until you can. If after 2 months I am still awaiting payment, you can consider your spot forfeit.

Turn Around Time: I plan on having my turn around time somewhere between 1-10 days depending on what is being asked for and the complication of the piece. I will give you an estimate of completion with it. If I fall behind on this, I will give you a discount code for your next piece.

Included Services: I offer 1 free resketch before a fee will be charged for more. This is due to my time and creative rights.

Use of art: Once the art is finished, you may do with it what you will. You paid me for your art, it is yours. Just please credit me.

What is not allowed in a request: I refuse to do any nudity as of yet. I will do vore, and some other things, but nothing overly sexual.

Reserved Rights: I reserve the right to cancel a commission at any time for any reason. I also reserve the right to deny any piece I do not feel comfortable working on or completing. Please do not be mad at me for these. I do this for the fun of it, and if I am not having fun with it, I don't want to waste my time being miserable.
